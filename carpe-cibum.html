Carpe Cibum

Food Waste Reduction Application

Software Engineering Design • Requirements Engineering • UX Design

Project Overview

An application that decreases food waste and increases food availability by selling reduced-cost food shortly before venues close, preventing restaurants from throwing out good, prepared food.

Software Engineering Requirements Engineering UX Design System Architecture

Carpe Cibum logo featuring sustainable design elements that represent the app's mission to reduce food waste.

Reduce Food Waste

Prevents venues from throwing out good, prepared food by connecting them with students

Affordable Food Access

Provides quality prepared food at reduced costs for budget-conscious college students

Late-Night Availability

Offers ready-made food options when campus dining closes early

Local Business Support

Fosters relationships with Miami-area restaurants, bakeries, and food businesses

Project Benefits

Students: Access to quality prepared food at reduced costs during late hours when campus dining is closed.

Businesses: Generate profit from food that would otherwise be discarded, reducing waste and increasing revenue.

Environment: Significant reduction in food waste, contributing to sustainability and resource conservation.

Stakeholders: Sustainable revenue model through small transaction percentages for app maintenance and growth.
