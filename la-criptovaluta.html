Cryptocurrency in Italian

Italian 435 Cultural Debates • Technical Communication • Cross-Cultural Presentation

For my Italian 435 Cultural Debates class, I had to create and deliver a presentation on a subject in my field of study (computer science). I chose to present on cryptocurrency, a tricky subject to explain in English, and admittedly more challenging than I could have imagined in a second language. Nevertheless, I had a fun time abstracting computer science concepts to the level of the everyday Italian speaker.
